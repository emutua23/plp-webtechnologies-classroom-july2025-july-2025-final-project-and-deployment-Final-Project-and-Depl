Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

General Questions

What services do you offer?

+

I specialize in UX/UI design, frontend development, prototyping, and design consulting. I can handle everything from initial concept and research to final implementation and testing.

How do you approach new projects?

+

Every project starts with understanding your goals, target audience, and requirements. I follow a structured process: Discovery → Research → Design → Prototype → Development → Testing → Launch.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely! I've worked with clients across different time zones. I'm flexible with meeting schedules and use collaborative tools to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.

Project & Pricing

How long does a typical project take?

+

Timeline depends on project scope. A simple website redesign might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

How do you structure your pricing?

+

I offer both project-based and hourly rates depending on the project type. For larger projects, I typically work on a milestone-based payment structure with 50% upfront and the remainder split across key deliverables.

What's included in your project deliverables?

+

Deliverables vary by project but typically include: research findings, wireframes, high-fidelity designs, interactive prototypes, style guides, and production-ready code with documentation.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes! I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in my standard packages. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate. I believe in iterating until we get it right.

Process & Collaboration

What tools do you use for collaboration?

+

I use Figma for design collaboration, Slack or Teams for communication, and project management tools like Notion or Trello. I'm flexible and can adapt to your team's preferred tools.

How do you handle feedback and iterations?

+

I present work at key milestones and gather structured feedback. I use tools like Figma comments or Loom videos to ensure clear communication and quick iterations based on your input.

Can you work with our existing team?

+

Absolutely! I enjoy collaborating with existing teams. I can integrate seamlessly with your developers, project managers, and stakeholders to ensure smooth project execution.

Technical Questions

What technologies do you work with?

+

For design: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Whimsical. For development: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, Vue.js, Node.js. I stay updated with the latest web standards and technologies.

Do you handle responsive design?

+

Yes! All my designs and development work is mobile-first and fully responsive. I ensure optimal user experience across all devices and screen sizes.

Do you consider accessibility in your work?

+

Absolutely! Accessibility is built into my design and development process from day one. I follow WCAG 2.1 guidelines to ensure your product is inclusive and accessible to all users.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes! I offer post-launch support including bug fixes, minor updates, and performance monitoring. I also provide documentation and training to help your team maintain the project.

Still Have Questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out! I'm happy to discuss your specific needs and answer any questions you might have.
